My name is Chloe and I'm an editorial designer with a passion for finding smart solutions to difficult design challenges.

Currently, I live in Washington, D.C. where I work as a designer and animator for Snapchat Discover at The Washington Post.

I recently graduated with a degree in graphic design from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. Most of my time there was spent at The Daily Orange, the independent student newspaper, where I designed and art directed for my first three years of college. The D.O. is where I fell in love with news design, and my work from there has been recognized in several competitions.
